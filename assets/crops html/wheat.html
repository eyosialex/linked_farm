üêõ Major Pests & Diseases

Common Problems

  • Stem rust: Use resistant varieties
  • Yellow rust: Fungicide application
  • Septoria: Leaf spot disease
  • Aphids: Vector for virus diseases
  • Armyworms: Early detection critical

üåæ Growth Stages & Management

Germination to Seedling

  • Duration: 7-10 days
  • Care: Ensure good seed-soil contact
  • Protection: Birds and soil crusting

Tillering Stage

  • Duration: 30-45 days after planting
  • Management: Top dressing application
  • Weed control: Critical at this stage

Heading to Flowering

  • Duration: 70-90 days after planting
  • Water needs: Critical for grain formation
  • Disease watch: Rust and mildew

üìÖ Harvesting & Threshing

Harvest Indicators

  • Grains hard and difficult to dent with nail
  • Straw turns yellow and dries
  • Moisture content: 12-15%
  • Timely harvest prevents shattering
  • Method: Cut close to ground or use combine
  • Timing: Morning hours to reduce shattering
  • Drying: Stack and dry for 3-7 days before threshing
  • Threshing: Mechanical or traditional methods
  • Storage: Clean, dry grains in rodent-proof bins
